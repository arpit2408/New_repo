Producers

Registered farmers, ranchers, greenhouse growers, viticulturists, and bee-keepers can use Hit the Target - The Crop Registry System - to enter crop locations, crop type, pesticide tolerances, and other information or enterprise, on an interactive digital map. Learn more

Pesticide Applicators/Consultants

Registered Pesticide applicators can view the location and details of crops or commodities surrounding pending application areas. Learn more

Flag the Technology

Flag the Technology is a field-marking program originally developed by personnel at the University of Arkansas1 and is now a widely accepted practice to reduce the risk of a misapplication. This practice will also make the applicator aware of sensitive crops adjacent to the field being sprayed.  Learn more
